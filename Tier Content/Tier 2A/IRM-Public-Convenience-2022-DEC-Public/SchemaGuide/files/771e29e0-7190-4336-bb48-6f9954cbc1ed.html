Attribute rr:RevisionRecallType / @rr:type
Namespace urn:us:gov:ic:revrecall
Annotations

Specifies the type of the revision or recall.
Type CVEnumRevRecallType
Properties
use required
Facets
enumeration ADMINISTRATIVE_RECALL
Notify recipients that a product has been withdrawn due to significant but non-substantive technical, legal, or policy issues (but is not used for a FISA-compliance recall); e.g., compromise of sources and methods, identification of US person, improper classification.
enumeration ADMINISTRATIVE_REVISION
Notify recipients that a modification has been made that does not affect the substance of the original product; e.g., misspellings, typographical errors, additional recipients.
enumeration FISA_COMPLIANCE_RECALL
Notify recipients that a product has been recalled specifically for a FISA-compliance reason.
enumeration SUBSTANTIVE_RECALL
Notify recipients that a product has been withdrawn due to significant concerns about the accuracy of the information, the integrity of the source, or the soundness of the analytic judgments.
enumeration SUBSTANTIVE_REVISION
Notify recipients that a change has been made that does affect the substance of the original product but does not invalidate it; e.g., dropped words or sentences, inaccurate paragraphs or comments, minor analytic judgments.

Attribute rr:RevisionRecallType / @rr:action
Namespace urn:us:gov:ic:revrecall
Annotations

Indicates the action to be performed upon receipt of the revision or recall.
Type CVEnumRevRecallAction
Properties
use required
Facets
enumeration PURGE
Immediately remove and destroy all copies of the revision of the product that has been revised or recalled.
enumeration RETAIN_WARN
Retain the existing copies of the revision of the product but indicate that it is not the latest revision.
enumeration RETAIN_HIDE
Retain the existing copies of the revision of the product but prevent access and distribution.
enumeration MANUAL_INSTRUCTION
The action to be taken as a result of the revision or recall requires manual intervention by a human to read and follow the included instructions.
